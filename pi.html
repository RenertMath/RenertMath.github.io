Renert π Day Challenge 2025

Finals: Thu, Mar 13 (K–8)  •  Fri, Mar 14 (Gr 9–12) — during regular math class

Event details

Finalists will compete in a common area, with a teacher checking digits. To be a finalist, you must place in the top 5 of your grade (K–6), or top 5 of Grade 7/8, or top 5 of Grades 9–12 during the qualifiers.

Rules

  • Be courteous and respectful. This is a fun event—unkindness will not be tolerated.
  • Students get 3 “strikes”. A strike only counts if the teacher must stop and correct you. If you self-correct immediately (e.g., “3.14159, 897— I mean 26535”), no strike.
  • Teachers will pause before confirming digits to allow quick self-corrections.
  • If you pause for more than 30 seconds, you’ll be prompted to continue; otherwise it counts as a strike.
  • We count decimal digits only: for example, 3.14 counts as 2 digits.

Prizes

To be determined.
