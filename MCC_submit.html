How to Find Your ZipGrade Student ID

Bright Minds & Renert School Instructions

Bright Minds Students

  • Login to your family portal.
  • Click My Account then Current Courses / Registrations.
  • You will find your name and BM Student ID number.
  • This 5-digit number is also your ZipGrade Student ID.

Renert School Students

  • The student ID you will use is the same as your Café student number.
  • If you do not know your Renert School Café Number, please email tutoring@renert.com.

How to Submit Your Answers

  • Click on TEST ONLINE for your session.
  • A ZipGrade page will open.
  • Enter your name and your ZipGrade Student ID.
