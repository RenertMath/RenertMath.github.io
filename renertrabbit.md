<h2> Renert Rabbit 2025 </h2>

<ul>
  <li><strong>What is Renert Rabbit?</strong>
    <ul>
      <li>Renert Rabbit is an in-house math competition for students currently in Grades 1-6.</li>
      <li>This contest is created entirely by the Math team at Renert School.</li>
    </ul>
  </li>
  <li><strong>When does it take place?</strong>
    <ul>
      <li>Renert Rabbit is held in February each year.</li>
      <li>Bright Minds students will write in-person in the Bright Minds building. (Sorry, no online option.)</li>
      <li>Details will be sent by near the date of the contest email.</li>
    </ul>
  </li>
  <li><strong>Why should my child write the Renert Rabbit math contest?</strong>
    <ul>
      <li>We always encourage students to try our math competitions because preparation for contests help build logical reasoning and analytical thinking skills.</li>
      <li>It also exposes them to a timed testing environment which many students this age have rarely experienced before.</li>
      <li>Our contest is completely for fun, and students should never be pressured into writing a competition.</li>
      <li>That being said, prizes will be awarded for top scoring participants.</li>
    </ul>
  </li>
  <li><strong>How can I help my child prepare to write the Renert Rabbit (or other math contest)?</strong>
    <ul>
      <li>Encourage your child to attend MCC sessions (see above). This is the best way to help them prepare.</li>
      <li>Working through previous math contest questions at home in a no-pressure environment and then attending the MCC zoom sessions to ask questions and see how the instructor solves the most challenging questions live with the group will.</li>
    </ul>
  </li>
</ul>
