Math Competition Club (MCC)

Math Competition Club (MCC) is back! for the 2025-26 school year!

How does it work?

  • Classes/Sessions are running weekly online on Saturdays/Sundays starting in the new year!
  • See schedule below for tentative dates, times, zoom information, and tests.
  • In order to attend MCC sessions, you are strongly encouraged to work on the contest problems to the best of your ability at home in advance.
  • It is OK to not answer everything. The instructors want to know which questions are more difficult, and focus on those during the class.
  • You can check our MCC-FAQ for more information.
