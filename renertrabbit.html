Renert Rabbit 2025

To access your answers in the bubble sheet from Renert Rabbit 2025

  • Go to the student portal: http://www.ZipGrade.com/student/.
  • Select the LEFT side (Login to Student Portal) with the ZipGrade ID.

If you are a Bright Minds student:

  • If your BM portal ID is a 5-digit number (starting with 5) → this will be your ZipGrade ID.
  • If your BM portal ID is a 6-digit number (starting with 51) → replace the first two digits (51) with 6 to obtain a 5-digit number.
    Example: if your BM ID is 510312, your ZipGrade ID will be 60312.

If you are a Renert School student:

  • Your ZipGrade ID is your Renert School café number (starting with 3).
